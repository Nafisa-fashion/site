Who are we?

We are two sisters who want to create a global fashion platform for Muslim women. Our vision is to make it easier for Muslim women to shop by building a platform for bloggers, brands and you!

Why?

We want to make it easy.

We felt that in the last couple of years, it’s become harder to dress 'modestly' so to speak. It stems from things like when you try and find a maxi skirt, you find it has slits, it’s short at the front or it is sheer. Ultimately, you have to start layering up in ways that you didn’t have to before or you just don’t wear them. It becomes hard work.

We want to make shopping for a modest wardrobe easy by providing inspirations and making outfits 'shoppable' .

" We aren't here to tell you what is or isn't modest, we are just here to make the journey a little easier."

We want to represent you.

Amaliah is also about fundamentally having a place for a community. It’s not that there isn’t choice out there for a muslim woman. It's that when you look at mainstream fashion sites and look at how models are styled, it can give the idea that we just don’t fit in or belong. A lot of what Amaliah is about is having a platform which really identifies with the core of being a Muslim woman, almost feeling ‘at home’ when you’re on our site.

We are here to discuss topics and ideas that everyday Muslims face.
