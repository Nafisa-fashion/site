Hello, My name is Zahra and I went to London Fashion Weekend for amaliah.co.uk.

Here’s my diary from #LFW16 it was the weekend event that was open to the public to shop designer brands (at a discount) and get the inside scoop on the latest trends.

Held at the beautiful Saatchi Gallery, off the King’s road, makes for a very fashionable back drop to the proceedings. So here’s the inside scoop.

On entering you get the opportunity to be a true celebrity - photo shoot style!(photo) and receive your complementary tote bag, filled with magazines and few treats that every fashionista needs including Lavazza coffee, Maybelline baby lips and Garner Skinactive moisture bomb.
